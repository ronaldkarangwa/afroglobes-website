About us

Welcome to AFROGLOBES! We are a full-service education consulting firm dedicated to helping students reach their educational goals. We provide personalized guidance and support to students and their families to help them maximize their potential and find success in their educational endeavors. Our team of experienced professionals is committed to helping our clients understand the college admissions process and develop strategies to maximize their chances of acceptance. We provide comprehensive college counseling services, from helping students select the right college to providing assistance with college essays and applications. We also offer personalized test preparation services to help students prepare for the SAT, ACT, and other standardized exams. Our experienced tutors and counselors provide personalized instruction and guidance to help students succeed on their exams.
